My Favorite Pictures From Last Term

This project is for cs52's SA1. If you want to check out the cs52 webpage, you can find it HERE.

If you would like to stay here, check out these super cool pictures! I took them while studying abroad in Cape Town last term! I am interested in medium format film and took a lot of pictures to learn more about it.

Below is a short gallery of my favorites as I was learning.

  1. Camps Bay Beach

    I took this picture on my last day in Cape Town. From this beach, you can see the 12 apostles of Table Mountain, but it was too cloudy to see them in this picture.

  2. The Rock

    I took this picture while watching the sunset from a place called "The Rock." A lot of people come here to picnic and watch the sunset! I didn't mean to take a picture of this couple but it worked out quite perfectly! I wish I could send this photo to them.

  3. Sunrise Beach

    I took this picture week 2 of the program. We wanted to explore all the beaches surrounding the city and found one called "Sunrise Beach." We did not make it there for sunrise, but it was so beautiful and nearly empty!

  4. South African Large Telescope

    This picture is not on film, but I wanted to include it because I love it. We visited the South African Large Telescope for a week to do observations - we would sleep during the day and stay up all night.
